## Design Context

### Users
CareerSense is for senior professionals, Directors, VPs, CXOs, and ambitious job seekers who are applying to high-stakes roles and want polished application materials without sounding generic.

### Brand Personality
Calm, executive, exacting. The product should feel premium, credible, private, and worth paying for as a subscription.

### Aesthetic Direction
Refined executive SaaS with a light-first interface, tinted ivory and blue-gray surfaces, deep ink typography, restrained teal accents, and subtle gold details. The interface should feel closer to a private career office than a generic AI tool.

### Design Principles
1. Lead with confidence, not decoration: fewer elements, stronger hierarchy.
2. Make AI feel controlled and professional: short labels, audit-like records, measured motion.
3. Use premium restraint: quiet surfaces, precise borders, refined shadows, no noisy gradients.
4. Optimize for executive trust: clear billing, templates, previews, and outcomes.
5. Keep workflows fast: every primary action should be obvious within seconds.
